The series was officially picked up by CBS on May 13, 2011,[8] and debuted on September 22, 2011.[9] On October 25, 2011, the show received a full season order.[10] It was renewed for a second season on March 14, 2012, by CBS, which premiered on September 27, 2012.[11] CBS renewed Person of Interest for a third season on March 27, 2013,[12]  with Sarah Shahi[13] and Amy Acker promoted to series regulars.[14] The series was renewed for a fourth season on March 13, 2014,[15] and was renewed for its fifth and final season on May 11, 2015.[4][16] ADR recording for the series was done at recording studio Cherry Beach Sound.[17] The music is composed by Ramin Djawadi.[18] The first season soundtrack was released on November 12, 2012.[19] The second soundtrack was released on January 21, 2014.[20] The third soundtrack, which contained music from the third and fourth season was released on January 29, 2016.[21]
